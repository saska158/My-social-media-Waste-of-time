# Perspective Score for Harassment

Perspective score runs from 0 to 1. For harassment, it is a moderately useful signal — targeted attacks are often toxic in tone, but coordinated pile-ons and cold targeting can score low.

## Thresholds

| Score | Interpretation for harassment |
|-------|-------------------------------|
| < 0.2 | Low toxicity — likely a cold/strategic attack or false report. Focus on targeting pattern, not tone |
| 0.2 – 0.4 | Low-moderate — check whether one user is being repeatedly singled out |
| 0.4 – 0.7 | Moderate — could be heated exchange or genuine harassment. Check whether one user is being singled out |
| 0.7 – 0.9 | High — aggressive tone confirmed. Check for repetition and whether a specific user is targeted |
| > 0.9 | Very high — severe tone. Verify targeting and check violation history before deciding |

## Adjustments

- Score is less reliable when the post is in Serbian, Bosnian, or Croatian — regional expression and sarcasm produce false positives
- A low score does not rule out harassment — repeated cold targeting of one user is harassment regardless of tone
- A high score in an obvious mutual argument is less meaningful than a high score in a one-sided attack
