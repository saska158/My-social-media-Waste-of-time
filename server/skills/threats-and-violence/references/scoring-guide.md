# Perspective Score for Threats and Violence

Perspective score is a useful but imperfect signal for this category. Explicit threats are often highly toxic in tone and score high. However, coded language, calm incitement, or glorification of past violence may score lower.

## Thresholds

| Score | Interpretation for threats |
|-------|---------------------------|
| < 0.2 | Auto-dismissed before you are called |
| 0.2 – 0.5 | Low toxicity — likely hyperbole or venting. Focus on specificity and target, not tone |
| 0.5 – 0.75 | Moderate — aggressive tone. Assess whether a specific person or group is named or implied |
| 0.75 – 0.9 | High — strongly aggressive. Check for specific target and prior violation history |
| > 0.9 | Fast-tracked — severe tone. Verify whether this is a specific, credible threat and act accordingly |

## Adjustments

- A specific named target is the single most important signal — it overrides score in both directions
- Calm, low-scoring posts that describe specific methods or targets should still be treated as high severity
- Regional hyperbole in Serbian, Bosnian, and Croatian is common — use community reaction in comments as a calibration signal
